The pgAdmin Development Team

Version 2.0, 25 July 2007

  1. Purpose

    The pgAdmin Development Team exists for 2 reasons:

    1. To manage and maintain the pgAdmin Project

    2. To ensure that pgAdmin remains freely available to anyone that wants it.

  2. Structure

    The foundation consists of the Project Leader and Members.

    1. Project Leader: The Project Leader is Dave Page, founder and primary developer of the software. The Project Leader is also considered a Core Developer.

    2. Members: Members can be any contributor to the project who accepts an invitation from the existing Development Team.

  3. Division of Responsibilities

    Any major decision regarding the Project must be voted on by the Development Team. Any vote will be made via email through the relevant mailling list. Responses not received within 2 weeks will be discounted from the vote.

    In the case of a hung vote, the Project Leader shall cast the deciding vote.
